About Me.

Hi, I’m Lily! Welcome to my little corner of the internet.

I am a photographer, videographer, filmmaker, and student based in Seattle, Washington. But most importantly, I strive to be a storyteller. I craft compelling narratives, expressive ideas, and authentic experiences together so that they can be shared through various creative mediums. Meeting unique people in exciting locations and picking up novel skills along the way is innately ingrained in my personality. I’m excited to try new things, and I’m excited to share everything with you!

If you have any questions please don’t hesitate to send me a message. Let’s Chat!

Cheers,
